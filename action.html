Veda's Story

Veda's relationship with food used to look very different. She stated that she barely ate breakfast most days, would always skip lunch, and eat pasta or takeout for dinner. However, she would snack a lot throughout the day, eating foods like Goldfish and Cheez-its. Her journey started when she was experiencing severe health issues. Her mental health took a big hit to the point that her anxiety and depression was getting so bad she had to stop everyday activities including hangingout with friends, going to school, or even going to the grocery store. Her physical symptons also took a toll on her as she was experiecning hair loss, bloating, and acne. Veda was later diagnosed with Lyme disease and she had to adjust her diet right away. She started focusings on eating nutrient-dense-foods and noticed that all her sympotons slowly went away. Energy levels went up, brain fog cleared up, and her anxiety felt less. Veda says that you shoudln't strive for perfection in your diet, but you need to listen to your body and give it food that emphasizes it ability and allows it to perform its best.

Danielle's Story

Through personal experience, Danielle realized that her food chouces greatly impact her mood, behavior, and mental health. When she was in middle school, Danielle was very overweight and felt depressed, sad, and grumpy most of the time. However, Danielle had envisioned wearing a gorgeous prom dress and realized she was motivated to improve her diet. She researched and evaluated food labels to see what foods would be most beneficial for her, specifically. After two years she had lost 80 pounds and felt more energized and confident than before. Danielle never expected to love physical activity, but overtime she gained a passion for running and when people ask where she gets the motivation from, she smiles and explains that eating nutrient-dense foods fuels both her physical and mental stamina. She also takes into consideration that mindset plays a role aswell. When her mindset changes so did her mental health drastically.
